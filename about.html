Meet Samuel

I'm the one-person operation behind Hailey Device Repair. When you text, I answer. When you hand me your phone, I fix it. No middlemen, no call centers, no corporate nonsense.

The Story

Why I started fixing things

I didn't start Hailey Device Repair because I had some grand business plan. I started because I was good at fixing things, people needed help, and the nearest Apple Store is two hours away in Boise.

I've always been the person friends call when their phone screen shatters or their laptop won't turn on. At some point I realized — if I'm doing this anyway, why not do it right? Proper tools, quality parts, real diagnostics. Not the "stick it in rice" kind of advice.

So here we are. A one-person repair shop in Hailey, Idaho, serving the Wood River Valley. Drop it off in Hailey or ship it from anywhere in Idaho. Same result: your device works again, you know exactly what I did, and it's covered for 40 days.

No storefront, no overhead, no inflated prices to cover rent on a strip mall. Just a guy with good tools, good parts, and a reputation to protect in a town small enough that word travels fast.

How I Work

Five things I won't compromise on

01

Honesty first

If your device isn't worth repairing, I'll tell you. I'd rather lose a sale than lose your trust.

02

Transparent pricing

You get a firm quote before I touch anything. No hidden fees, no "well, we found additional issues." The price I quote is the price you pay.

03

Quality parts

I don't use the cheapest parts from the cheapest supplier. Every screen, battery, and component I install is tested before it goes in your device — and warranted for 40 days after.

04

Your data is yours

I don't snoop through your phone. I don't copy your files. If a repair requires a factory reset, I'll tell you first and help you back up. Your privacy matters.

05

Speed without shortcuts

Most repairs are done same-day, many within the hour. But I won't rush and risk doing it wrong. Quick and right — not quick and sloppy.
