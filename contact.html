Common Questions

Before you reach out

How quickly will you respond?

Texts usually get a reply within 15–30 minutes during business hours. Emails within a couple hours. Forms within the same day. If it's after hours, you'll hear from me first thing the next morning.

Do I need to know what's wrong with my device?

Nope. "My phone screen is cracked" or even "my laptop is being weird" is totally fine. That's what the diagnosis is for — I'll figure out exactly what's wrong and explain it in plain English before quoting anything.

Is the quote really free?

100% free, always. I'll diagnose the issue, tell you what it'll cost, and give you my honest opinion on whether it's worth repairing. If you decide not to fix it, you owe nothing. Zero. I don't charge for looking.

What if I'm not in Hailey?

No problem. If you're anywhere in Idaho, I offer mail-in service with free return shipping. I've even worked with folks in Montana and Oregon.

Can you fix my device while I wait?

For most screen replacements and battery swaps — yes, usually under an hour. More complex repairs (water damage, motherboard issues, data recovery) may take a few hours or overnight. I'll always give you a time estimate upfront.

Do you accept walk-ins?

I work by appointment to make sure I have the right parts ready and can give your device my full attention. But scheduling is quick — just text me and we can usually set something up same-day. No formal booking system, just a text.

Still not sure? Just text me.

Seriously — even if you just want to ask "is this worth fixing?" That's a valid question and I'm happy to answer it.
