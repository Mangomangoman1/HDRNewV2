    build What's involved

    • Front glass panel
    • LCD/OLED display assembly
    • Digitizer calibration
    • Adhesive seal replacement
    • Earpiece speaker test

    format_list_numbered The process

    1. 01 Heat and soften the adhesive seal
      iOpener or heat gun at 70°C loosens the factory adhesive without damaging the display
    2. 02 Carefully separate glass from the frame
      Thin pry tool slides between glass and frame, releasing each clip along the edges
    3. 03 Disconnect display cables from logic board
      Metal shield removed, three ZIF connectors released with plastic spudger
    4. 04 Install new display assembly
      OEM or OEM-quality replacement display seated, connectors secured, shield replaced
    5. 05 Reconnect, calibrate, and test everything
      Multi-touch test, True Tone re-enrollment, ambient light sensor calibration, drop test

    FAQ

    Common questions, straight answers

    Everything you need to know before dropping off (or mailing in) your device.

    How long does a typical repair take? expand_more

    Most phone repairs — screens, batteries, charging ports — are done same day, often within 1–2 hours. Laptop repairs typically take 1–2 business days depending on parts availability. I'll give you a clear timeline before starting.

    Do you offer a warranty on repairs? expand_more

    Yes. All repairs come with a 40-day warranty covering parts and labor. If the same issue comes back within that window, I'll fix it free of charge.

    Is my data safe during the repair? expand_more

    Absolutely. I never access personal data during repairs. Your photos, messages, and files stay private. For repairs that require a wipe (rare), I'll always warn you first so you can back up.

    What if you can't fix my device? expand_more

    Every repair starts with a thorough diagnosis. I'll explain what's wrong and give you a firm quote before any work begins — no pressure, no obligation.

    How does mail-in repair work? expand_more

    Simple: text or email me to describe the issue and get a quote. I'll send packing instructions, you ship it to me (any carrier), I repair it, and ship it back with tracking. I text you updates through the whole process. Works for anyone in Idaho.

    Can you fix water-damaged devices? expand_more

    I can try, and often succeed. Success depends on severity and how quickly you bring it in. The sooner the better — and please don't put it in rice (it doesn't help). Just text me ASAP and I'll walk you through what to do.

    Do I need an appointment? expand_more

    Nope. Just text me at (208) 366-6111 and we'll work out a convenient time. Just text me and we'll work out the most convenient way to get your device to me.
