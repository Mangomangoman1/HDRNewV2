iPhone FAQ

Questions about iPhone repair

How much does iPhone screen repair cost in Hailey? expand_more

iPhone screen replacement starts at $59, with the final price depending on your specific model. Newer models with OLED displays cost more than older LCD models. I'll give you an exact price before any work starts — every repair starts with a thorough diagnosis.

How long does an iPhone screen replacement take? expand_more

Most iPhone screen replacements are done in 30–60 minutes. I keep common screens in stock for popular models. For less common models, I may need to order the part (usually arrives next business day).

Do you use genuine Apple parts? expand_more

I use OEM-quality aftermarket parts that match Apple's specifications for fit, display quality, and touch sensitivity. They're indistinguishable from original parts in daily use and come at a significantly lower cost. Every part is covered by my 40-day warranty.

Will I lose my data during the repair? expand_more

No. Screen replacements, battery swaps, and most hardware repairs don't touch your data at all. Your photos, messages, apps — everything stays exactly as it was. In the rare case a repair requires a reset, I'll always warn you first.

My iPhone got wet — what should I do? expand_more

Don't put it in rice. That's a myth and can actually make things worse. Instead: power it off immediately, don't plug it in, and text me ASAP at (208) 366-6111. The faster you get it to me, the better the chances of a full recovery. Water damage repair starts at $49.

Can you fix my iPhone if I'm not in Hailey? expand_more

Absolutely. I serve Ketchum, Sun Valley, and Bellevue locally, and offer mail-in repair for all of Idaho. Just text me, I'll send packing instructions, you ship it, and I'll have it back to you with tracking. Updates via text the whole way.
