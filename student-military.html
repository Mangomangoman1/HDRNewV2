Questions

Common questions

How much is the student/military discount? expand_more

15% off the total repair cost. Applied to every service — screens, batteries, ports, upgrades, tune-ups, diagnostics (already free), everything. No minimum spend, no exclusions.

What counts as valid student ID? expand_more

Any current student ID from a college, university, trade school, or high school. A .edu email address works too. International student IDs (ISIC) are accepted. If your school ID is expired but you can show current enrollment (schedule, tuition receipt), that counts.

What military/veteran ID is accepted? expand_more

Active-duty military ID (CAC), veteran ID card, VA health card, DD-214, National Guard or Reserve ID, and retired military ID. Military spouses with a dependent ID qualify too.

Can I combine this with other promotions? expand_more

The 15% student/military discount stacks with our 40-day warranty (which always applies). It cannot be combined with other percentage-based promos like seasonal sales. You'll always get whichever discount is larger.

Does this work for mail-in repairs? expand_more

Absolutely. Just mention your student or military status when you text us and snap a photo of your ID. The 15% will be applied to your final total. Mail-in repairs still include free return shipping.

Is there a limit on how many times I can use it? expand_more

Nope. Use it every time you need a repair. Break your screen twice a semester? (It happens.) You get 15% off both times. The discount lasts as long as your student or military status is valid.
