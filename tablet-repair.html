Know Your Tablet

iPad vs Android: repair differences

Different builds, different repair approaches. Here's what matters for your tablet.

tablet_mac

Apple iPad

Screen: Most iPads use a fused glass+LCD/LED assembly held by strong adhesive. Removal requires precise heat application. iPad Pro uses Liquid Retina (more expensive panel).

Battery: Glued in with pull tabs. Not user-replaceable. Lasts 2-3 years of heavy use before noticeable degradation.

Repair time: 1–2 hours for screen, 1 hour for battery. The adhesive is the time-consuming part.

tablet_android

Samsung Galaxy Tab

Screen: S-series uses Super AMOLED (gorgeous but pricey). A-series and FE use LCD (more affordable). AMOLED screens cost 2× what LCD screens cost.

Battery: Similar adhesive mounting to iPad. Samsung uses slightly more accessible internal layout in most models.

Repair time: 1–2 hours for screen. Samsung adhesive is usually a bit easier to work with than Apple's.

local_fire_department

Amazon Fire

Screen: LCD screens across the board. Generally the most affordable tablet screens to replace. Build quality is simpler — designed for value.

Honest take: Fire 7 and HD 8 are so inexpensive new ($50–$100) that screen repair only makes sense if you want to save data or have the Kids Edition with accessories.

Fire Max 11: Worth repairing — nicer hardware, better display, higher replacement cost.

Tablet FAQ

Questions about tablet repair

How much does iPad screen repair cost in Hailey? expand_more

iPad screen replacement starts at $79 for standard iPad models. iPad Air is typically $89–$109, and iPad Pro with Liquid Retina display starts at $129. I'll give you an exact quote based on your specific model — every repair starts with a thorough diagnosis.

Can you fix a cracked iPad that still works? expand_more

Yes — and it's usually cheaper when the LCD underneath is fine. The glass digitizer (the part you touch) can sometimes be replaced separately from the display. Even if you see lines or dead spots, the display assembly can usually be replaced. Text me a photo and I'll give you a quick assessment.

Do you repair Samsung tablets and Amazon Fire? expand_more

Absolutely. Samsung Galaxy Tab (S-series, A-series, FE), Amazon Fire (7, HD 8, HD 10, Max 11), Lenovo, Microsoft Surface Go — I repair all major brands. Same thorough diagnosis, same 40-day warranty, same commitment to honest assessment.

How long does a tablet screen replacement take? expand_more

Most tablet screens take 1–2 hours. Tablet screens are adhered with strong adhesive (unlike phones that use clips), so careful heat separation is needed. The precision work takes time, but it's worth it — a rushed tablet screen repair leads to dust under the glass.

Is it worth repairing an older iPad? expand_more

If it still gets software updates (iPad 7th gen+, iPad Air 3+, all iPad Pro 2018+), repair almost always makes sense. $79 for a screen vs $329+ for a new iPad is easy math. For iPads no longer getting updates (iPad 6th gen and older), I'll give you an honest recommendation — sometimes the money is better spent toward a newer model.

My kid dropped the iPad — can you fix it today? expand_more

Probably yes. I keep common iPad screens in stock. Text me the model (go to Settings → General → About) and I'll confirm same-day availability. I also sell tempered glass screen protectors and can recommend the best kid-proof case — so next time it survives the drop.
